Q2. Let say I have, some string "hello this side regex"
    Find out the count of the total vowels
â—‹ vowels - ['a','e','i','o','u']
ANS:-
	string="hello this side regex"
        vowels = ['a','e','i','o','u']
	count=0
	l=list()
	for i in string.replace(" ",""):
    		l.append(i)
	for k in l:
    		if k in vowels:
        	count+=1
	print(count)
